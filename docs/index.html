The life and times of Beau Harrison

Beau has an undergraduate degree is physics from Henderson State University. He is currently a Senior Accelerator Operator at Fermi National Accelerator Labortory. He is also pursing a degree in Public Policy from the Harris School at the University of Chicago.

Eduation

While attending Henderson, Beau worked on classifying cataclysmic variable stars using a remote telescope. From the skills learned from this exercise he and his peers wrote middle school curriculum to guide classes in observing the night sky using a remote telescope.

He has worked with an administrative baord in implementing the Proton Improvement Plan. He is now working to implement Machine Protection Systems for new accelerator projects.
